Who We Are

A Section 8 Non-Profit Built on Collective Action

InAmigos Foundation was founded by Mr. Govind Shukla with a clear mission: to unite minds and create measurable social change. What began in Chhattisgarh now spans 28 states, powered by over 200 dedicated volunteers.

We run six structured programs covering food security, education, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental conservation, and skill development — reaching over 50,000 beneficiaries across India.

✔ Section 8 Registered ✔ 80G & 12A Certified ✔ CSR-1 Registered ✔ NITI Aayog Registered ✔ ISO 9001:2015

Our Mission

Empower marginalised communities through structured volunteer-led action and transparent corporate partnerships.
